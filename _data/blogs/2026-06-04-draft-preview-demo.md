This post is saved as a **draft**, so it is **not visible** to the public.

It exists only to show how **preview mode** works:

- On the normal blog page it does **not** appear.
- On the preview link (`/blog/?preview=1`) it **does** appear, with a "Draft · not public" badge, and opens with a preview banner.

When you're happy with how a real post looks here, you simply uncheck **Save as draft** in the CMS and it goes live. This demo post is safe to delete.
