### Understanding the Needs of Your Family

Traveling requires careful planning to account for unique individual needs, sensory sensitivities, and preferences that help ensure smooth experiences.

### Planning Your Trip

Research autism-friendly destinations offering quiet spaces and sensory-friendly options. Involve family members in planning to reduce anxiety and build excitement.

*Recommended destinations:*

- Myrtle Beach, South Carolina
- Beaches Resorts (Caribbean)
- Atlantis Paradise Island, Bahamas
- Morgan's Wonderland – San Antonio, Texas
- Sesame Place – Langhorne, Pennsylvania and San Diego, California
- LEGOLAND Florida Resort – Winter Haven, Florida
- SeaWorld (Orlando, San Antonio, and San Diego)

Contact destinations in advance for specific accommodation details.

### Preparing for the Journey

Assemble travel kits with favorite snacks, toys, and comfort items. Practice travel scenarios (packing, airports, public transport) to familiarize family members and reduce anxiety.

### During the Trip

Maintain flexible schedules while having structured plans. Use visual aids and schedules to communicate activities and reduce uncertainty.

### Finding Support

Connect with local autism support groups and community resources at your destination for advice and assistance.
